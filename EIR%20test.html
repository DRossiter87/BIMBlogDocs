1.2   Outcomes

1.2.1.C    The appointing party is intending to produce an exemplar asset information model demonstrating the application of relevant international standards.

1.2.2.C    The appointing party's vision for this project is:

Simplicity through Standardization.

To achieve this vision, these exchange information requirements have been produced to ensure that information delivered by the lead appointed party:
  • Complies with ISO 19650-2;
  • Complies with any relevant national, European, and International Standards around the production of information, technical documentation and document management; and
  • Is limited to information required to support the specified purposes.

  • Asset register;
  • Support for renovation;
  • Predicted and actual impacts;
  • Operations;
  • Maintenance and repair; and
  • Replacement information.
